My Journey: From Code to Technical Analytics Implementation

My journey in the world of digital analytics began as a freelance developer while I was simultaneously pursuing my BSc in Data Science. During this time, I worked on developing tools related to Adobe Analytics and Adobe Launch, which not only honed my technical skills but also sparked a deep interest in web analytics. The experience of creating solutions that directly impacted data collection and reporting opened my eyes to the power of analytics in shaping business decisions.

Driven by this newfound passion, I transitioned into web analytics, working with multiple clients to implement tagging solutions and design robust data layers. Each project added a layer of expertise and practical knowledge, and I quickly became proficient at translating complex business requirements into efficient, scalable analytics implementations.

My career took a significant turn when I joined Petco. Over the course of two years, I immersed myself in large-scale analytics projects, optimizing Adobe Launch properties, streamlining tag management in GTM, and implementing e-commerce marketing tags across various platforms. My consistent delivery, problem-solving abilities, and technical acumen led to my promotion to Technical Lead, where I had the opportunity to manage a team and mentor peers, further enhancing my leadership skills.

Eager to grow and explore next-generation Adobe tools, particularly in the Adobe Experience Platform (AEP), I sought new challenges and found an exciting opportunity at Lenovo. Joining a team that had won the Adobe Experience Maker Award in 2025, I began collaborating with Adobe Analytics champions, contributing to innovative solutions, and further elevating my expertise in enterprise-level analytics.

Today, I stand at the intersection of digital analytics, web development, and AI/ML, blending technical knowledge with strategic insights to drive data-driven business outcomes. My journey has been fueled by curiosity, continuous learning, and a passion for transforming complex data into actionable insights—a journey that continues as I explore the next frontier of analytics and technology.
